# Commercial Use Policy (Plain English)
This document explains what **is and is not allowed** under the CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license used for textures and image assets in this project.

## ❌ NOT Allowed (Commercial Use)
You may NOT:
* Sell these textures or assets
* Include them in paid Foundry VTT modules
* Use them in products, games, or apps that are sold
* Monetize them directly or indirectly (including asset packs, Patreon rewards, etc.)

## ✔ Allowed (Non-Commercial Use)
You MAY:
* Use these assets in personal games
* Use them in free Foundry modules
* Modify and remix them for non-commercial purposes
* Share them freely with proper attribution

## ⚠️ Gray Areas
If money is involved in ANY way, it is likely considered **commercial use**.

Examples:
* Patreon-supported modules → ❌ Not allowed
* Free module with optional donations → ⚠️ Risky
* Streaming content using assets → usually ✔ allowed
